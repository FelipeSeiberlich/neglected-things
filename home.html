Our Strategy

ABOUT


Have you ever failed achieving any personal goals? Can you name anything you are neglecting right now in your life? Do you remember any of your New Year's resolution that got lost? If your answer is YES to any of these questions, you are in the right place.


Neglected Things had its inspiration in a famous board game called Chess. No panic! This website is not about the game itself. The inspiration comes from its strategy, pieces movements and how we can align them to solve problems related to the important things we neglect in our lives.

STEPS


If you ever played a Chess board game you already know that Strategy is key for success. The ability of antecipate the "moves" in the game, as obstacles in our lives are importante to keep a good flow. The chess game will be used merelly to illustrate our strategy.


Like in Chess, your first move should be always forward and made by the most basic piece - the Pawn. When we have a personal goal set, or when we decide to look after something we are neglecting we have a tendency to speed it up the process or even to break the rules to get it done. Remember one step at the time is your first step!
